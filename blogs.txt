First-time buyer resources in 2026

Long Beach buyers should also look into assistance programs early. The City of Long Beach points buyers toward federal, state, and local homebuyer assistance resources, including CalHFA programs. CalHFA offers several homebuyer loan programs for California buyers, including programs for first-time homebuyers.

CalHFA also notes that first-time buyers may need to complete a homebuyer education course, depending on the program. The California Dream For All program is designed for eligible first-generation homebuyers and can provide down payment or closing cost assistance through a shared appreciation loan structure.
